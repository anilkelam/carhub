Range Rover Velar

Inside, the Velar offers a luxurious and contemporary cabin, with meticulously crafted materials and innovative design elements. The Touch Pro Duo infotainment system, featuring dual touchscreen displays, seamlessly integrates with the vehicle's controls and provides access to a range of features, from navigation to entertainment.
